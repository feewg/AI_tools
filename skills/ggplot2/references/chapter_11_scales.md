# Scales Overview

Scales control the mapping from data to aesthetics (size, colour, position, shape) and provide interpretation tools (axes and legends).

## Scale Categories

**Position scales and axes** - Chapter 10
- Continuous and discrete position scales
- Log scales, reverse scales
- Date/time scales

**Colour scales and legends** - Chapter 11
- Continuous colour gradients
- Discrete colour palettes
- Colour blindness considerations

**Other aesthetics** - Chapter 12
- Size scales
- Shape scales
- Linetype scales
- Alpha scales
